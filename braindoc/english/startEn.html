Hello and welcome to my website!

What is this website about?

Music:
I produce electronic music, mainly techno and (hard-)trance, sometimes also gabba, chill-out and since some time also dark-electro. I started roughly in 1994 with "Scream-Tracker", later I migrated to "Fast-Tracker", then one day I bought the Soundblaster AWE32 and even later the professional hardware-synth Roland JX-305. But today I use this synth almost exclusively as MIDI-keyboard, since today I prefer to work with software-synthies and effects (mostly in the form of VST-PlugIns).

Sounddesign and Programming:
Not only do I produce music, but I'm also a passionate synthesizer- and effect-freak - this means I'm interested in sounddesign and all this technical stuff and signal-processing which is involved there. For this reason one day I started to develop my own VST-PlugIns, many of which you can download as freeware under the menu-option "VST".

Private:
My real name is Robin Schmidt, I was born in 1979 and live in Berlin, Germany. If you want to, you can view some funny party-pictures under the menu-option "PICS" - but this area is obviously more intresting to me and my friends than to the public.
