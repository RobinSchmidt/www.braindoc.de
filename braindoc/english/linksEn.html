Websites of friends:

www.dark-hour.de
A radio-transmission on the open channel berlin in which I'm involved as audio-engineer.

www.powerofmusic.de
The website of my friend Dario, who is also the mastermind of the radio-transmission mentioned above.

www.berlin-electro.de
This is a site about the EBM-/Electro-/Darkwave-Scene in Berlin. There are many party-pictures, an event-planner and a lively forum in which I also sometimes hang around.

www.true-spirit.de
Well...sad... the Tresor is history now. At least in it's old location. When, where and how the story will go on, you will get to know here - it's not over! Greets to Rundstutzen - great site!

http://www.andy-rockabillies.de/
Website of the Rock'n'Roll-Band "Andy & The Rockabillies". Frontman "Andy" is a friend of mine who also plays guitar. Sometimes we make music-sessions and combine electronic and rock elements.

www.flying55.de
Flying 55 is a EBM band from Berlin. Some time ago we produced a song called "Körperwelten" together with Andy.

Clubs in Berlin:

www.tresor-berlin.de
As mentioned above - this club doesn't exist anymore. But their website is still with us.

www.mfe-berlin.de
The "Ministerium für Enstpannung" which means: "Ministry of Relaxation" in English. A very relaxed club indeed. Nevertheless, the music is asskicking. Goa-Trance!

www.k17.de
The K17 club. At the moment the best club for dark-musik in Berlin. In particular regarding the concerts. For 10-15 Euro you can get there artists who could easily fill large concert-halls.
